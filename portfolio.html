Illustrations

For my UI/UX Design class my group had to make an app that was one of a kind. So my group member and I created an app that would bring the past to you. Our app won third place in the national BEA Festival of Media Arts Competition in the category of Emerginig Media and Technology. Timestrider would allow you to view information of historical buildings based on location or through our search function. It even allows you to view the building and what it used to be with AR functionality. We created this in hopes that we could learn about some of our forgotten past and bring light to what used to be and what exist now. Enjoy!
